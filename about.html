About Us

About Us

Welcome to NutriFarm, where we bring you the freshest and most natural farm products directly from our fields to your table.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide nutritious and sustainably-grown food while promoting responsible farming practices and supporting our local community

We adhere to organic farming principles, avoiding harmful pesticides and fertilizers. Our commitment to sustainable agriculture ensures that we protect the environment while providing healthy food options.

History

NutriFarm was established in 2020 by a group of four people Rakhi, Sanika, Keertana and Soniya with a vision to create a sustainable and self-sufficient farm that respects nature. Over the years, we've grown from a small family-run operation to a thriving farm known for its high-quality produce.

We are proud to support our local community through various initiatives, including partnerships with local schools and participation in farmers' markets.

We are more than just a farm; we are a community. We host regular farm tours, workshops, and events to connect with our customers and share our passion for sustainable living. By supporting NutriFarm, you are not only enjoying fresh, healthy food but also contributing to a more sustainable future.
